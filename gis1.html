Convenience Store Robbery/Shooting in Coniston
With ESRI ArcGIS Pro

In this project, I utilized ArcGIS Pro to analyze a series of convenience store robberies and shootings that occurred in Coniston. Within a span of 37 minutes, six convenience stores were targeted, with two incidents resulting in shootings.

The map I created showcases the locations of the six convenience stores involved in the robberies, along with nearby bridges. Upon receiving a 911 call reporting suspicious activity on one of the bridges, I employed geoprocessing and spatial analysis techniques in ArcGIS Pro. By creating buffers around the convenience stores and analyzing the intersections of roads and bridges, I identified which bridges required investigation by the marine unit.

Through this project, I demonstrate my proficiency in ArcGIS Pro and spatial data analysis, contributing valuable insights to law enforcement efforts.
