Personalized Case Study on Daily Acitivities
With R Markdown and ggplot

In this case study, I meticulously analyze my daily activities, including hours of sleep, class attendance, studying, exercising, and more. Leveraging statistical methods such as Confidence Interval, One Sample t-test, and Two Sample t-test, I gain valuable insights into my routine. Using R Markdown, I present my findings in a structured and reproducible format. Additionally, I enhance data visualization with ggplot, providing clear and insightful graphical representations of the analysis.

In this section, conducted one-sample hypothesis tests for the three variables: hours spent studying, sleeping, and exercising. These tests helped us determine if there were significant differences between the sample means and hypothesized population means.
