China Reigns as Global Cigarette Market in 2014
With Visme

This infographic highlights China's dominance in the global cigarette market in 2014. The global cigarette industry, known for its profitability and harmful effects, witnessed a significant increase in retail value, soaring to 744 billion USD from 1009 to 2014.

Divided into four parts, it showcases the massive scale of the global cigarette industry, discusses global market share, and illustrates China's overwhelming dominance in cigarette volume, nearly eight times larger than its closest competitor, Russia. Additionally, it demonstrates how China's volume surge fuels global market growth.

From this infographic, we gain valuable insights into the scale and influence of China in the global cigarette market, emphasizing the need for awareness and action to address the harmful effects of smoking worldwide.
